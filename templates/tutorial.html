With Unlock 4/90 you can choose the hospital center to pickup your therapy from.

To join this service, go to the hospital center that follows your health journey and tell them you want to signup to Unlock 4/90.

After the signup you will be automatically matched with the hospital center of your choice.

When it's time to pickup your therapy, go to the "Pickup" section to choose a time.

La tua richiesta sarà presa in carico e la tua terapia sarà inserita in uno smart locker nei pressi della farmacia ospedaliera.

When the day of the pickup comes, go to the hospital center with your device.

Find the smart locker and show the QR code or type the pickup code that was given to you to pick up your therapy.

One of the smart locker doors will open and you will be able to pick up your therapy in total privacy, without waiting in a queue!
